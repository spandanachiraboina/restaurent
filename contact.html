Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a dress code? +

We have a smart-casual dress code. We ask that guests avoid sportswear, flip-flops, and overly casual clothing. We want you to feel comfortable and look your best.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? +

Absolutely. We cater to vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and most allergy requirements. Please mention this in your reservation notes so our kitchen can prepare accordingly.

What is your cancellation policy? +

We kindly request 24 hours' notice for cancellations. For private events and the tasting menu, we ask for 48 hours' notice. Late cancellations may be subject to a fee.

Is valet parking available? +

Yes, complimentary valet parking is available from 6:00 PM daily at the main entrance. We also have a dedicated car park adjacent to the restaurant.

Can we bring our own wine? +

We do not permit corkage. However, our sommelier has curated an exceptional cellar to suit all occasions and budgets. We'd love to find the perfect bottle for you.

Do you host children? +

Ember & Oak is an adult-oriented fine dining environment. We welcome children aged 10 and above during dinner service. Please speak to us when booking so we can accommodate you appropriately.
