Our Story

Crafted with
Passion & Fire

Ember & Oak was born from a singular obsession: to serve food that tells a story. Chef Arjun Mehta trained across three continents before returning home to create a restaurant that honours the soul of Indian cuisine while embracing the world.

Every dish is a study in contrast — the char of the grill against the velvet of a slow braise, the heat of spice tempered by the cool clarity of fresh herbs.
